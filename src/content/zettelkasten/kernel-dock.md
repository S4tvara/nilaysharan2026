Kernel Dock maps a practical architecture for operators who need speed, auditability, and low maintenance overhead.
It stays close to terminal workflows and keeps the moving parts small.

Why this note exists:
I want defaults that prevent expensive mistakes before they happen.
The command surface should stay composable for shell-first usage.

Current build direction:
Structured logs tuned for incident debugging
Modular adapters so integrations stay optional
Queue-aware scheduling for burst traffic
