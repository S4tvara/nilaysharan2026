---
title: Dreams
date: 2026-03-21
---

Dreams are the most private experiences a person can have. A dream journal is perhaps the only record meant never to be shared.

In dreams, the mind moves without restraint. Memories, fears, and fragments of thought surface without the filtering that governs waking life. It is the closest place to the inner core of individual consciousness.

If an entity were able to control a person's dreams, it would gain access to that innermost sanctum. From there, influence would no longer need to act on the external world.

It could shape the mind itself.
